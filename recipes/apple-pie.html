Ingredients

  • pie crust
  • apples
  • sugar
  • all purpose flour
  • lemon juice
  • cinnamon, nutmeg, salt

Steps

  1. Press one pie crust into the pie plate. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for a few minutes while you prepare the filling.
  2. Make the apple pie filling from scratch by stirring together the apples, sugar, flour, lemon juice, and seasoning.
  3. Transfer filling to crust.
  4. Place second pie crust on top of fruit.
  5. Crimp the edges of the crust, then cut slits in the top to vent.
  6. Bake at 425Â° F for 40-45 minutes, until the apples are tender.
